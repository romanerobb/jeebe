About Jeebe

Mataso Jean-basile aka Jeebe was born on September 23, 1997, in Kolwezi which is located in the Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC). He is a musician, primarily in the hip-hop genre. He became interested in music at around eight (8) years old. Some of his inspirations are: My Staury and Leka. Jeebe was signed to Robb Entertainment Corporation/Robb Music Group in 2021.
